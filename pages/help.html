System Documentation

LNI v2.1 Terminal Help

Full Sheet Inventory

  • Navigate to Sheets > Full Stock to view standard material inventory.
  • Use the top filter bar to narrow results by Material Type, Thickness, or Heat Number.
  • To allocate material, click RESERVE. This updates the status across all console instances.
  • To add new stock, use the + Add Sheet button. Ensure the Heat Number matches the physical MTR.

Sheet Cropper (Remnants)

  • Navigate to Sheets > Cropper for partial sheet/remnant tracking.
  • Search results are primarily sorted by Dimensions to help find drops for small jobs.
  • When a remnant is used, click REMOVE to delete it from the cloud database.
  • When adding a crop, input exact Width x Length measurements in inches.

General System Operations

Cloud Syncing

The console uses a real-time background sync. Changes are pushed to the master Google Sheet automatically. Check the status indicator in the top header to ensure you are ONLINE.

Cross-Machine Usage

The console is web-based. Always refresh (F5) if the terminal has been idle to ensure the local cache matches the master database.

System Warning

Do not manually edit the Google Sheets while the console is open to avoid data overwrites.

Operator Wellness & Support

Managing Shop Stress

High-output environments can be taxing. Utilize these coping mechanisms:

  • Tactical Breathing: 4 seconds in, 4 seconds hold, 4 seconds out to reset the nervous system.
  • Micro-Breaks: Step away from the screen or machine for 60 seconds every hour.
  • Task Triaging: Focus only on the immediate next cut or entry if the queue feels overwhelming.

Need to Talk?

Mental health is just as critical as shop safety. Our HR representatives are available for confidential support regarding anxiety, burnout, or personal stress.

Recommendation: Contact your HR Representative immediately if you feel overwhelmed or need resources.
